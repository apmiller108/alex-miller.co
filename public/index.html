MIXXX MIDI CLOCK

Arduino-based MIDI clock generator that bridges the gap between Mixxx (digital DJing software) and external hardware gear. It synchronizes tempo between tracks playing in Mixxx and external hardware like drum machines, sequencers, and samplers through MIDI clock signals.

Also provides transport controls that are beat aware for squencer like behavior.

The device can also function as a standalone MIDI clock generator, offering flexibility for various performance scenarios.
